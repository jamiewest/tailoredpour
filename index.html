FAQ

Quick answers for booking and setup. If you have a unique venue rule, just tell us — we’ll tailor around it.

Do you provide alcohol?

No — alcohol is provided by the host. We can provide non-alcoholic beverages (coffee, refreshers, mocktails) and the tap setup for client-supplied kegs where permitted by the venue and local rules.

Do we need a bartender?

Most events run smoothly without a dedicated bartender because the system is designed for fast self-serve flow. Some venues require staffed service — if yours does, we’ll coordinate options and keep it simple.

How much power do you need?

One standard grounded outlet within ~25 feet is ideal. If your venue is outdoors, we’ll plan an extension option.

How long does setup take?

Typically 20–30 minutes depending on tap count and station layout. Teardown is usually faster.

Where do you serve?

Inland Empire + surrounding areas. Travel fees may apply outside the core service zone.
